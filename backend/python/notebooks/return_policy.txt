RETURN & REFUND POLICY
======================
Last Updated: April 2026
This document outlines our complete return, refund, and exchange policy.

======================================
GENERAL RETURN POLICY
======================================

1. All products can be returned within 30 days of purchase date.
2. Items must be in original condition with original packaging.
3. Proof of purchase (receipt or invoice) is mandatory for all returns.
4. Returns must be initiated by contacting our support team first.
5. Do not ship items back without receiving a Return Authorization Number (RAN).

======================================
CATEGORY SPECIFIC RETURN RULES
======================================

ELECTRONICS
- Electronics can be returned within 15 days if unopened and in original packaging.
- Opened electronics can be returned within 15 days only if defective.
- All accessories, manuals, and cables must be included in the return.
- Data on devices is customer responsibility — we are not liable for data loss.
- Damaged screens, physical damage, and liquid damage are not eligible for return.
- Gaming consoles must be returned with all controllers and cables.
- Laptops must have all stickers and seals intact for non-defective returns.

CLOTHING & FOOTWEAR
- Clothing items can be returned or exchanged within 30 days.
- Items must have original tags attached and be unworn and unwashed.
- Innerwear, socks, and swimwear are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.
- Footwear must be returned in original box without signs of outdoor use.
- Sale items can be exchanged but not refunded.
- Sunglasses must be returned with original case and cleaning cloth.

FOOD & BEVERAGES
- Food and beverage items are NON-RETURNABLE once opened.
- Unopened food items can be returned within 7 days if damaged or expired.
- Perishable items (milk, eggs, bread, fruits) are non-returnable.
- If food product causes health issues, contact us immediately with batch number.
- Canned and packaged goods can be returned if seal is intact and within expiry.

KITCHEN ESSENTIALS
- Kitchen appliances can be returned within 30 days if unused and in original packaging.
- Used appliances can be returned only if defective within warranty period.
- Cookware with signs of use (scratches, stains) cannot be returned.
- Non-stick coating damage due to misuse is not eligible for return.
- All parts, accessories, and manuals must be included in return.

BOOKS & STATIONERY
- Books can be returned within 30 days if in original condition.
- Books with annotations, highlights, or torn pages cannot be returned.
- Stationery items can be returned within 30 days if unused.
- Notebooks with written pages cannot be returned.

======================================
REFUND PROCESS
======================================

1. Contact support team to initiate return and receive Return Authorization Number.
2. Pack item securely in original packaging with all accessories.
3. Include copy of original invoice inside the package.
4. Ship to our return center address provided by support team.
5. Refund is processed within 7 business days of receiving the returned item.
6. Refund is issued to original payment method only.
7. Cash refunds are not available — refund goes back to original payment source.
8. Shipping charges are non-refundable unless item was defective.

======================================
DAMAGED ITEMS
======================================

1. Inspect all items immediately upon delivery.
2. Report damaged items within 48 hours of delivery.
3. Photograph the damage before opening packaging.
4. Submit photos along with order number to support team.
5. Damaged items are eligible for full refund or replacement.
6. Return shipping for damaged items is fully covered by us.
7. Replacement for damaged items ships within 3-5 business days.

======================================
EXCHANGE POLICY
======================================

1. Exchanges are available for clothing and footwear within 30 days.
2. Exchange is subject to availability of desired size or color.
3. Price difference applies if exchanging for higher priced item.
4. Exchanges for electronics only available for same model in case of defect.
5. Exchange shipping cost is covered by customer for non-defective items.
6. Only one exchange allowed per order.

======================================
NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
======================================

The following items CANNOT be returned under any circumstances:
- Opened food and beverage products
- Perishable goods (milk, eggs, fresh produce)
- Innerwear, socks, and swimwear
- Digital products and software licenses
- Customized or personalized products
- Items marked as Final Sale
- Hazardous materials

======================================
BULK ORDER RETURNS
======================================

1. Bulk orders of more than 50 units require manager approval for returns.
2. Bulk return requests must be submitted in writing via email.
3. A 10% restocking fee applies to non-defective bulk returns.
4. Processing time for bulk returns is 14 business days.
5. Partial bulk returns are evaluated on case by case basis.
6. Bulk food orders are non-returnable once delivery is accepted.

======================================
CONTACT FOR RETURNS
======================================

Phone: Available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM
Response time: Within 24 hours on business days
Return Authorization Number (RAN) required before shipping any return.
